Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about JobTrackr.
Is there a free plan? ▾
Yes! Our free plan lets you track up to 20 job applications with full Kanban board access, basic analytics, and email reminders — no credit card required.
How does the AI resume analyzer work? ▾
Upload your resume and paste a job description. Our AI (powered by Claude) scores your resume against ATS systems, identifies missing keywords, and gives you specific, actionable improvements tailored to the role.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▾
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your profile settings — no questions asked. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Is my data secure? ▾
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use JWT authentication, secure API validation, rate limiting, and never sell your personal data to third parties.
Does it support multiple languages? ▾
JobTrackr currently supports English and Arabic, with more languages coming soon. You can switch languages from your profile settings.
